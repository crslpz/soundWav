How to Use soundWav


  • Choose either a sample listed by genre or create your own melody with chords(you may not choose both).
  • The sequencer is color coded to match 4/4 time signature.
  • To create a pattern, click either the navy or burgundy boxes and once complete press the play button.
  • To switch the kind of drum sound click the icons to the left of the sequencer. They will toggle from white to pink depending on the source. Refer to the icons to the right -->
  • Pressing the Play/Stop button will begin the pattern from the beginning
  • To effectively use the vibe selector, please stop the sequencer, select the vibe and press play.
  • If you use the Piano patterns(the last 3 icons on the sequencer) along with a preset sample, the sample will take precedent. To use the paino keys, de-select the sample.
  • Try the soundbytes by clicking the icons on the right.
